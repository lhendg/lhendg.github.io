Modern Family Data Visualization

Posted by on June 30, 2020

I just finished watching Modern Family a few days ago and to be totally honest, I cried my eyes out while watching the last episode. Now that I have nothing to watch, at least for now, might as well create a project out of it.

First, I would like to mention that this project was inspired by Isabella B's the community project.



Using Requests and BeautifulSoup, I scraped the data from Modern Family's iMDB page. I did a few clean-ups on formatting, but overall, data was pretty clean already. Meanwhile, I created my visualization in Tableau. I wanted to do a lot more visualization but I decided to reserve that for another show, so watch out for that.
